Lisa Nakahara Historian

Lisa is a PhD student in the Higher Education and Student Affairs program at the University of Iowa. Her work focuses on history of women in science and higher education. She is currently researching science education in Japanese and American (girls') teacher training schools in the late 19th and early 20th centuries.
